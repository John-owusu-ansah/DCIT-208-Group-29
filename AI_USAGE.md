# AI Usage Log

## AI Usage Policy

Artificial Intelligence tools may be used to assist with brainstorming, documentation, code explanation, testing ideas, and project planning.

All AI-generated outputs must:

* Be reviewed by a human team member.
* Be verified for correctness.
* Be modified where necessary.
* Be properly documented in this log.

## Team AI Contract

The team agrees that AI tools are assistants and not replacements for individual effort. All submitted work remains the responsibility of the team members. Any AI-generated content used within the project will be reviewed, validated, and disclosed appropriately.
